# Privacy Policy for FreshKeep

**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025

## Introduction

FreshKeep ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, and profile photo (via Google Sign-In)
- **Household Data:** Household name, member information
- **Inventory Data:** Food items, expiration dates, categories, and quantities you add to your fridge, freezer, or pantry
- **Barcode Data:** Product barcodes you scan and associated product information
- **Voice Data:** Voice recordings for expiration date input (processed locally and not stored)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features you use, timestamps of actions
- **Analytics Data:** Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
- Sync your inventory data across household members
- Send notifications about expiring items
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Prevent fraud and ensure security
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- **Firebase Firestore:** Your data is stored securely on Google's Firebase platform
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Access Control:** Only authenticated household members can access shared inventory data
- **Local Storage:** Some data is cached locally on your device for offline functionality

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

- **Household Members:** Inventory data is shared with members of your household
- **Service Providers:** Firebase (Google Cloud), OpenFoodFacts API (for product lookups)
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, database, and notifications
- **OpenFoodFacts:** Public product database for barcode lookups
- **Google Sign-In:** Authentication service

Each service has its own privacy policy:
- Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- OpenFoodFacts: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your information
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Export:** Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-Out:** Disable notifications or voice input features

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hdunlap936@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy

FreshKeep is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Push Notifications

We send push notifications to alert you about:

- Items expiring soon
- Household invitations

You can disable notifications in your device settings or in the app settings.

## Voice Data

When you use voice input for expiration dates:

- Voice data is processed locally on your device
- Recordings are NOT stored permanently
- Voice recognition is powered by your device's native capabilities (Apple/Google)

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups:** Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.
